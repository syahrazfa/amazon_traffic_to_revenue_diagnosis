## Amazon Customer Behaviour Verdict

### Problem

Modern E-Commerce platforms generate massive product catalogs that appear to perform well at surface level (sales, ratings, discount volume), but many of these products silently destroy contribution margin through excessive discounting, weak trust leverage, and low elasticity.

Traditional dashboards describe activity.
They do not diagnose economic survival.

This system exists to answer one question:

> Which product families should be scaled, fixed, or eliminated to protect capital?
